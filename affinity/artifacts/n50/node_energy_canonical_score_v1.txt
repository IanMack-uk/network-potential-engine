Job 11 — Canonical score declaration (v1)

Canonical score (frozen for v1)
- Canonical score for downstream ranking/export is: E_eff

Definitions
- E     := beta0*s + beta1*v
- E_eff := beta0*s + beta1*(rho ⊙ v)

Frozen parameters (v1)
- beta0 = 1.0
- beta1 = 1.0
- rho policy: rho_i = 1 for all i

Notes
- Under the v1 rho policy, E_eff == E, so rankings coincide.
- E_eff remains canonical to preserve a stable downstream interface when a nontrivial rho is introduced later.
